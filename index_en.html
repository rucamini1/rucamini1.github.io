Our History

Moments of dedication and miracles achieved through God's guidance.

Starting a Pioneer Church with 20 Million Won

In May 2013, our church was established in Songdo New City with a deposit of 10 million won and about 8 million won spent on self-interior design, made possible by the heartfelt dedication of young adults and pastors.

Miraculous First Baptism of 16 Souls

In October 2013, through consistent Bible studies and evangelistic meetings, 16 precious souls were reborn as children of God, marking our first major milestone as a pioneer church.

Reborn as an Internet Mission Church

Amid the COVID-19 crisis, we transformed into an internet broadcasting church, spreading the gospel globally through live worship broadcasts, overseas mission interviews, and various YouTube programs.
